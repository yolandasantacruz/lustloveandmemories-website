Lust, Love, and Memories is a compilation of poems that focuses on all stages of romantic relationships, from the desire and excitement felt at the very beginning, to the aftermath of them.

From the first flutter of attraction to the quiet ache of absence, each poem in this collection captures a distinct emotional landscape. Yolanda Santa Cruz writes with raw honesty and lyrical precision, offering readers a mirror for their own experiences of longing, connection, and loss.

This book invites you to revisit every stage of love — not as a timeline, but as a constellation of moments that shape who we become.
